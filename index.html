About Me

I am a fifth year graduate student in the Physics Department at UC Davis working with Maruša Bradač. I study galaxies in the first billion years after the Big Bang using gravitational lensing and large telescopes. I am passionate about outreach and inclusion efforts. When not working, I like to spend free time outside.
